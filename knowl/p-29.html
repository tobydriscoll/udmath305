Playing the role of a point on the line is a particular solution \(y_p(t)\text{.}\) As the name implies, it is any one solution of the original ODE (1.3.1). In places the text uses \(y_{vp}\) to specify a special "very particular" solution that satisfies the constraint \(y_{vp}(0)=0\text{.}\)
