Definition 1.4.1. Matrix times matrix.

If \(\bA\) is \(m\times n\) and \(\bB\) is \(n\times p\text{,}\) then the product \(\bA\bB\) is defined as

\begin{equation} \bA\mathbf{B} = \bA \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{b}_1 \amp \mathbf{b}_2 \amp \cdots \amp \mathbf{b}_p \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \bA\mathbf{b}_1 \amp \bA\mathbf{b}_2 \amp \cdots \amp \bA\mathbf{b}_p \end{bmatrix}\text{.}\tag{1.4.1} \end{equation}
