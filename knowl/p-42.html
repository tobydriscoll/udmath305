The unit step function or Heaviside function \(H(t)\) is zero for \(t\le 0\) and one for \(t\gt 0\text{.}\) It represents throwing on a switch. The solution when \(q(t)=H(t)\) is called the step response and follows from the formula as \(y(t) = \frac{q}{a}\bigl( e^{at}-1 \bigr)\text{.}\)
