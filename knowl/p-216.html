The characteristic roots are now conveniently \(-Z \omega_n \pm \omega_n \sqrt{Z^2-1}\text{.}\) We see that the condition for being underdamped is simply \(Z \lt 1\text{.}\) In this case we express the roots as \(-Z \omega_n \pm \omega_d\text{,}\) using the damped frequency \(\omega_d=\omega_n\sqrt{1-Z^2}\text{.}\)
