It's worth mentioning here the related idea of a window function, which turns on and then back off. To get a forcing that is one only between times \(S\) and \(T\text{,}\) for example, we can use \(f(t)=H(t-S)-H(t-T)\text{.}\) The formulas for the window solution become a bit messy, so it's best to just work out results from each step separately.
