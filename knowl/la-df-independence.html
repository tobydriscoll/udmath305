Definition 1.6.3. Linear independence.

The vectors \(\bx_1,\dots,\bx_k\) are linearly dependent if there are scalars \(c_1,\ldots,c_k\text{,}\) not all zero, such that

\begin{equation*} c_1\bx_1+\cdots+c_k\bx_k = \bzero\text{.} \end{equation*}

Otherwise the vectors are linearly independent.
