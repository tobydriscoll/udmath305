If \(q=A \cos(\omega t) + B\sin(\omega t)\) and you don't want to recast it into shifted cosine form, you get a messier equivalent result:
\begin{equation*} y_p = (G \cos \alpha)[ A \cos(\omega t) + B\sin(\omega t) ] + (G \sin \alpha)[ A \sin(\omega t) - B\cos (\omega t)]\text{.} \end{equation*}
