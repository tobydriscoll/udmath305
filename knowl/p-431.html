If \(\bV\) is nonsingular, we therefore can write

\begin{equation*} \bA = \bV \bD \bV^{-1}\text{,} \end{equation*}

which is called a diagonalization of \(\bA\text{.}\) A diagonalization is not unique; after all, we can take the eigenvalues in any order, and the eigenvectors themselves are not unique.
