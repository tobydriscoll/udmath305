Exercise 3. Laplace transform of cos and sin.

Assume that our previously derived \(\lx[e^{ct}] = 1/(s-c)\) remains true when \(c\) is imaginary. (Also assume that \(s\) remains real.) Take the real and imaginary parts of both sides and apply the linearity of the transform to deduce formulas for \(\lx[\sin(\omega t)\) and \(\lx[\cos(\omega t)]\text{.}\)
