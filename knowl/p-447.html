The equation \(x''+x=\cos(t)\) is supposed to suggest the particular solution \(x_p=A\cos(t)+B\sin(t)\text{.}\) But look at what happens after substitution:

\begin{align*} [-A\cos(t) - B\sin(t) ] + [ A\cos(t) + B\sin(t)] \amp =\cos(t)\\ 0 = \cos(t)\text{.} \end{align*}

This leads us nowhere good.
