Solution 4.6.5.3.1.
\begin{align*} \lim_{\omega\to 0} \lx^{-1}\left[\dfrac{As+B}{s^2+\omega^2}\right] \amp = \lim_{\omega\to 0} \left[ A\cos(\omega t) + \dfrac{B}{\omega}\sin(\omega t) \right] \\ \lx^{-1}\left[ \frac{A}{s} + \frac{B}{s^2} \right] \amp = A + Bt \\ A + B \lx^{-1}\left[ \frac{1}{s^2} \right] \amp = A + Bt \\ \lx^{-1}\left[ \frac{1}{s^2} \right] \amp = t. \end{align*}
