Example 2.6.1

The oscillator \(4y'' + y' + 36y\) from an earlier example has \(\omega_n=3\) and \(Z=1/\sqrt{16\cdot 36}=1/24\text{.}\) Accordingly, gain is maximized when \(\omega/\omega_n=1-\frac{1}{288}\approx 0.9965\text{,}\) so for practical purposes a resonance occurs at just slightly less than the natural, undamped frequency.
