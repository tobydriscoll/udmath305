You should be familiar with the idea of multiplicity for roots of a polynomial. We can factor a characteristic polynomial \(p\) to get

\begin{equation*} p(z) = (z-\lambda_1)^{m_1}(z-\lambda_2)^{m_2}\dots(z-\lambda_k)^{m_k} \end{equation*}

for nonnegative integer exponents such that \(m_1+\cdots+m_k=n\text{.}\) These exponents are the multiplicities of the roots.
