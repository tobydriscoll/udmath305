Recall our claim that the fundamental solution \(g(t)\) of the impulse forcing problem \(ay''+by'+c=\delta(t)\) leads to the particular response to any input \(f(t)\) via \(y_p(t)=\int_0^t g(t-s)f(s)\,ds\text{.}\) Also recall that we can fudge finding \(g\) by having no forcing, \(g(0)=0\text{,}\) and \(g'(0)\) becoming instantly nonzero.
