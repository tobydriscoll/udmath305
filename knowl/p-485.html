As with linear problems, nonlinear ODEs of order higher than one can be converted to first-order problems in a higher dimension. Therefore we can organize the discussion entirely around first-order problems. In the first few sections we limit ourselves to scalar (or one-dimensional) problems in the form \(x'=f(t,x)\text{.}\) Then we turn our attention to systems of two equations in two variables. The extension to still higher dimensions is closely related, but more complicated.
