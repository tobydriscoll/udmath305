In total the forcing function has eight terms, which we can group into four pairs. These lead to the solution parts

\begin{equation*} x_n(t) = \frac{1}{r} H(t-6n) \Bigl(1 - e^{-r(t-6n)}\Bigr) - \frac{1}{r} H\bigl(t-6n-\tfrac{1}{12}\bigr) \Bigl(1 - \exp\bigl(-r(t-6n-\tfrac{1}{12})\bigr) \Bigr) \end{equation*}

The homogeneous part of the solution is again zero, so the total solution is \(x(t)=3\sum_{n=0}^3 x_n(t)\text{.}\)
