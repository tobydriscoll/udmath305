For complex numbers \(w\) and \(z\text{,}\)

  1. \(\displaystyle |\bar{z}| = |z|\)
  2. \(\displaystyle |wz| = |w|\cdot |z|\)
  3. \(|w+z|\le |w| + |z|\) (triangle inequality)
  4. \(\displaystyle \left| \frac{1}{z} \right| = \frac{1}{|z|}\)
  5. \(\displaystyle \overline{wz}=\bar{w}\cdot \bar{z}\)
  6. \(\displaystyle \overline{w+z}=\bar{w}+\bar{z}\)
  7. \(\displaystyle \overline{\left(\frac{1}{z} \right)} = \frac{1}{\bar{z}}\)
  8. \(\displaystyle |z|^2 = z\cdot \bar{z}\)
