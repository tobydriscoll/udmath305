We next want to examine the linear oscillator in standardized form (4.4.1) when there is an exponential forcing function present:

\begin{equation} x'' + 2 Z \omega_0\, x' + \omega_0^2\, x = e^{rt}\text{.}\tag{4.5.1} \end{equation}

We're typically interested in the undamped and underdamped cases, \(0\le Z \lt 1\text{.}\)
