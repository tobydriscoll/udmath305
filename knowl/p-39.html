It's often useful to introduce a delay into the switch. To have the forcing turn on at time \(T\gt 0\text{,}\) we use \(H(t-T)\text{.}\) The response becomes

\begin{equation*} y(t) = \frac{q}{a}H(t-T) \bigl[ e^{a(t-T)}-1 \bigr]\text{.} \end{equation*}

The step remains in the solution because nothing happens until the switch is turned on.
