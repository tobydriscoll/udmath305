It's straightforward to get the Laplace transform of a step function.

\begin{align*} ${\cal L}[H(t-T)] \amp = \int_0^\infty H(t-T) e^{-st}\, dt \\ \amp = \int_T^\infty e^{-st}\, dt \\ \amp = \left[ \frac{e^{-st}}{-s} \right]_c^\infty = e^{-ct}\frac{1}{s}\text{.} \end{align*}

More generally, you can follow the same trail to get

\begin{equation*} {\cal L}[H(t-T)f(t-T)] = e^{-sT} {\cal L}[f]\text{.} \end{equation*}
